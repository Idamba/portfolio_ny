Nordsjællands Kattehjælp

Nordsjællands Kattehjælp is a network of volunteers who strive to help stray cats in Denmark. They both help stray cats into new families, as well as neutralise strays and release them back into the streets. The project was made for my second semester exam.

In this project we decided to work with scrum to become further acquainted with it, and hopefully learn more about it along the way.
This costumers allowed us to work with headless CMS and API through wordpress once again, and as seen further down we successfully executed this.

You can use the tabs below to see the different steps i had a part to play in.

User analysis

As usual I started off with a categorization to find out the target audience. Here it became evident that we had two separate target audiences, since the website would be used both by visitors interested in adopting, as well as visitors who had a cat in need of help, and needed information about what to do.

Other than that the target audience would be from Zealand and above 18, as these are demands stated by the organization.

From this categorization we did 5 interviews, where we learned the following: It's important for visitors interested in adopting to know about the organization and the people behind it, just as much as it is important for them to get information about the cats. Another thing mentioned was how it should be quick and easy to donate.

After this we did a competitor analysis to get an overview over what the site might be lacking. We used kattens-vaern.dk and foreningensimba.dk for this.

Scrum

In this project we worked with scrum once again. I first established the information architecture by doing a card sorting to get an idea of the users mental model.

Other than that I worked quite a bit with the design of the website, mainly focusing on optimizing UI from the first prototypes that were created by another group member. Here I made use of basic design principles, such as the gestalt principles and Dan Browns eight principles of information architecture.
