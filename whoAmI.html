Who am i?

Thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio! My name is Ida-Marie, and I'm a 22 year old woman studying multimedia design. Ever since I was a child I've been an unique and creative soul, always seeking out new experiences due to my curious nature.

Professional life

In my professional life I'd like to see myself working as an UX/UI designer, but I also have an interest in frontend development, and wouldn't mind working a bit with that as well.
Through my education at UCN I have learned to work in all areas of multimedia design ranging from project management, user analysis, UX design, UI design, coding, photography and more. Other than that I have basic skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript as well as Adobe programs such as Adobe XD, lightroom, photoshop and illustrator.

I'm fluent in Danish and English.

Personal life

As a person I'm a proper geek who enjoys videogames, anime and everything in between. In my free time I'm a cosplayer, which for me means I'm very creative in general and love trying out new techniques and testing new theories in all aspects in life. I'm also an educated nail technician, which adds further to my creative and experimentalist soul.

On top of that I'm a very social person, and love interacting with all kinds of people in all aspects of life.

User analysis

Throughout my education I have worked with interviews, categorization, user tests and more. These are used to thoroughly examine who the target audience should be, and how I can best accommodate them.

Project management

Another thing that UCN has taught me is how to use project management for all kinds of projects. I always make use of WBS, Gantt-diagrams, prototyping, scrum and more, depending on the size of the project.

UX/UI design

When I develop a website I always keep the users needs and interaction patterns in mind. I have lots of experience from my education working with the users expectations in mind.
